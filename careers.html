Our values

Stay curious

We are innovators at heart. Fuelled by shared excitement and passion, we aim high and are constantly building the next “big thing(s)”.

Keep it simple, keep it clear

Clarity affords focus and with focus comes conviction and true originality. Whether for our customers, partners or colleagues, we deliver solutions that are clear, easy to use and intuitive.

We take care of each other

We’re a tight-knit bunch and we take care of each other, putting kindness and empathy above our own egos. Before acting, we always ask ourselves, am I doing either "more good" or "less harm".

Joy creates joy

With Nature, things don’t always work out quite the way you planned. This path of exploration and discovery brings us joy that we want to share with the whole world. We like to shake up the routine and bring fun to serious topics.

The perks of being a Click & Grower

Make a green dent in the universe

Every day, you’ll help people across the globe help the planet and live a healthier, mindful, and happy life.

Rewarding challenges

Making the future happen is a fast and rewarding ride. We’re a big family in small and agile teams. You’ll play an integral part in everything we achieve and always have the chance to grow in Click & Grow.

The green package of joy

You’ll get the perks of our start-up grade offices that are as green and full of life as they get. You can enjoy sporting benefits, health boosting food, tree planting and other team events. And you’ll definitely have the chance to become a gardener and the greenest person your friends & family know.
